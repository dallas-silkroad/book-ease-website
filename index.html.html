10 / FAQ

Real answers, no fluff.

Is your AI replacing the human bookkeeper?
No. AI handles the heavy lifting — categorization, anomaly detection, forecasting — but every entry is reviewed by a certified human before close. The combination is the point: machines for speed and pattern recognition, humans for judgment and strategy. You get both.
What if my books are a complete mess right now?
That's the most common starting point. Cleanup is a one-time engagement priced separately based on the work — usually 30–90 days of historical reconciliation. After that, you transition into a monthly plan and the AI keeps your books from drifting again.
Do you do my taxes too?
We handle quarterly estimates, sales tax, and tax-strategy work continuously throughout the year. For year-end filing we coordinate directly with your CPA and hand them a clean, audit-ready package — or we can refer you to one of our trusted CPA partners.
How is my data secured?
Bank-grade encryption in transit and at rest. Role-based access controls. AI processing happens in isolated workspaces — your data is never used to train public models. We follow SOC 2-aligned operational standards and undergo periodic third-party security review.
Do you only work with QuickBooks?
QuickBooks Online is our primary platform — we're certified ProAdvisors and have 15+ years of expertise. We also work with Xero for clients who prefer it. If you're on Wave, FreshBooks, or something else, we'll migrate you as part of onboarding.
How quickly can we start?
Discovery call within 48 hours. Audit delivered in week one. Most clients are fully transitioned with dashboards live inside three weeks. We onboard a limited number of new clients per quarter so each engagement gets proper attention.
What's the smallest business you'll work with?
Our Essential tier serves businesses doing $20K–$80K/mo. Below that, you're probably better served by a tool like QuickBooks Self-Employed or a part-time freelancer. We're built for businesses that have scaled past the DIY phase and need real financial infrastructure.
